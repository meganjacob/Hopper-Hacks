What are the Three R's?

The garbage patch is about the size of Texas north or Hawaii. Plastic takes centuries to break down and even then it only gets broken down to tiny pieces of plastic. The ocean animals constantly mistake it for food or a home. Sea turtles, dolphins and other animals get tangled up in heaps of plastic. It makes up 60-90% of all marine debris.
