  • Plant trees

  • Compost

    • Composting is another way to use kitchen and yard waste instead of it just ending up in a landfill somewhere. Composting also is useful as fertilizer since it enriches soil by adding nutrients to the soil and introducing organisms that can help decompose organic material.

  • Use other methods of transportation then cars

    • Not using cars can reduce greenhouse gas emissions and also improve your gas mileage which saves you money. Walking, biking, or taking public transportation helps to decrease greenhouse gases, too, and helps you burn more calories.
